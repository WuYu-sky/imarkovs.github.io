Ivan Markovsky obtained MS degree in Control and Systems Engineering from the Technical University of Sofia in July 1998 and Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering from the Katholieke Universiteit Leuven in February 2005. From January 2007 to September 2012 he was a lecturer at the School of Electronics and Computer Science of the University of Southampton. Since October 2012 he is with the department ELEC of the Vrije Universiteit Brussel. His current research interests are structured low-rank approximation, system identification, and data-driven control.
