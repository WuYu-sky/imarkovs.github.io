References:

  • Feedback Systems: An Introduction for Scientists and Engineers, K. Astrom and R. Murray

    • Background reading on control theory.

  • Introduction to linear algebra, G. Strang

    • An excellent and very readable text for lecture 1.

  • Introduction to mathematical systems theory: A behavioral approach, J.W. Polderman and J.C. Willems

    • Advanced text covering the material of lectures 3-9 (in more details).

  • Computer-controlled systems: theory and design, K. Astrom and B. Wittenmark

    • Covers the material of lectures 6-9.

Prerequisites:

  • MATH 1013, MATH 1017, MATH 2021, and MATH 2022 (linear algebra and differential equations)
  • ELEC 1011: Communications and control (dynamical systems, convolution, transfer function)
  • ELEC 2019: Control and systems engineering (frequency domain control methods)
  • MATLAB (short tutorial and a longer one)

Grading: 100% on written exam
