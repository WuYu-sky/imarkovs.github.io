Course information

Lectures take place on 1, 8, 15, 22, and 29 February, and 14 March, from 13:00 to 15:00, in room 3021, building 13.

Lecturer Ivan Markovsky, Building 1, room 2029, Tel. 8059 8715, im@ecs.soton.ac.uk

Course work: there is no official requirement for course work. Small assignments/challenges will be given during the lectures.

Prerequisites: knowledge of linear algebra at the level of G. Strang, Introduction to Linear Algebra, Wellesley Collegeis, 1998 is assumed. There is no requirement for past exposure to numerical computations and optimization.

No subscription is required for this course. Students who plan to attend the full set of lectures are kindly asked to send and email to the lecturer mentioning their background and research interests.
