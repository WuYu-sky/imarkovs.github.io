Linear algebra with applications

School of Electronics and Computer Science

University of Southampton

"Linear algebra with applications" is a graduate level course, given in the framework of the ECS Mathematics Training Courses (ECS password required). It is a revised version of the course "Numerical linear algebra and optimization" given in the 2007/08 academic year. The optimization part of the 2007/08 version of the course will be given as a separate course.

Course information

Lectures 18, 19, 20, 21, and 22 January, 9:00-11:00, in building 6, room 1083. No subscription is required for this course.

Lecturer Ivan Markovsky

Prerequisites: knowledge of linear algebra at the level of G. Strang, Introduction to Linear Algebra, Wellesley College, 1998 is assumed.
