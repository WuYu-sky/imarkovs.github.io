• TITLE: Software for structured total least squares estimation
  • AUTHORS: I. Markovsky, S. Van Huffel, and Rik Pintelon
  • ABSTRACT: The Matlab function stls.m solves the multivariate structured total least squares problem, in which the extended data matrix is partitioned into blocks and each of the blocks is block-Toeplitz/Hankel structured, unstructured, or noise free. The computational complexity of the algorithms is O(m), where m is the sample size. Requires Matlab 6.0 or later and the Mathworks' Optimization Toolbox. For the crutial computations stls.m calles functions from the SLICOT library.
  • KEYWORDS: parameter estimation; total least squares; structured total least squares; system identification; model reduction.
  • STATUS: Available on-line.
